## 2024-03-12 - Missing ARIA Labels on Icon-only Buttons
**Learning:** Found multiple instances where icon-only buttons (like close buttons or message action buttons) lacked descriptive `aria-label`s, making them inaccessible to screen readers.
**Action:** When adding or reviewing icon-only buttons, always ensure an explicit `aria-label` is provided in Indonesian, consistent with the local language of the app.
